ABOUT US

We envision Fine Arts Club as an open platform for all kinds of artists to be able to practice any and all mediums they want.
We see the club as a platform where people of different art style and artistic backgrounds get to exchange their ideas and artworks.
We strive to consistently improve the level of work at Fine Arts Club and at the same time showcase the talent at different inter/intra college events.

RECENTS EVENTS

  1. Freshers' Night
    This occasion marks the first contact point between freshers and the club. Various art performances are given by freshers' batch on this day which provides a platform to exhibit their talents.
  2. Workshops
    Fine Arts club is dedicated to offering a free and helpful service to the IIT Kanpur campus students and faculty, to those interested in art, providing a showcase for amateur artists. Apart from free service the club is also a big platform for those who want to learn and develop skills and ideas.
  1. Inter IIT/College Events
    The Fine Arts Club annually takes part in multiple inter college events (eg. Inter-IIT etc.) and is able to achieve many laurels.
  2. Art Fest
    ArtFest is the annual flagship event organized by Fine Arts Club. The purpose of the event is to bring art out of the club room and on to the streets to make it engaging for all. As a part of the 'festivities', several installations are made on the theme and placed at popular spots across the campus with the idea of making art interactive.
