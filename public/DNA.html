    • ABOUT US

    Introduction - “This is how we define ourselves”

    Design and Animation Club is a place where all the design and animation enthusiasts can meet and exchange their crazy artistic ideas. A place to practice state of the art design and animation techniques where people can explore their artistry. A place to call 'home' for our fellow designers and animators. And a place to exercise creativity without any bounds, where we get praised and understood for even our little but commendable efforts.

    Vision - “Practising Design for the Art of it.”
    We will consistently learn design and animation software and use them to manifest our imagination. And organize workshops, provide projects, and a climatic exhibition for showcasing all the work completed during our tenure.
