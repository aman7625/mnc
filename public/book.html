ABOUT US

“I have always imagined that paradise will be a kind of library.”
The quote hits us just right but you don’t need to keep imagining such heaven; because we at the Book Club, aim to bring down that paradise- loaded with a diverse collection of books and not to mention, comfy bean-bags for the feel of it. Whether you are new to the world of books or a dedicated bookworm, we got your back covered. Our collections include Fiction, Non-Fiction, Science Fiction, Fantasy, Classics, Poetry, Drama, Collections, Comics, Popular Science, Self Help, Romance, Biographies, Autobiographies, and even regional novels in Bengali and Hindi.
This year, the Club released its first-ever podcast in accord with the IITK Podcast Project. This podcast talks about the tropes, class authors, their august style, screen adaptations and other general regards of Detective Fiction. We intend to continue on this by covering other genres as well. In general, we conduct events that range from novel-based theme competitions and fun get-togethers to our flagship event, The Halloween Party! The SAC Book Club is maintained and operated by a team of 3 coordinators and 14 secretaries who work on a completely voluntary basis. During the semester, the club opens 7 days a week, for 1 hour on weekdays (9-10pm) and 2 hours on the weekends (8-10pm).
Do drop by and we can have our little, adventurous trips to new worlds!
