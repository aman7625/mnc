ABOUT US

The Debating and Discussion Society(popularly known as DebSoc) is IITK’s sole inter-collegiate, competitive debating group. The team competes in Asian(AP) and British(BP) parliamentary debate formats and has enjoyed success in the national debating circuit in the recent years.
On campus we conduct regular debates and talks/discussions. Members walk away from such sessions with a greater understanding of how to make and understand arguments. More importantly, however, they develop a more comprehensive view of the world around them and improve their ability to think critically ; an ability which sets them apart from their peers in life. The members also support each other in their endeavours and often serve as a personal and professional support group. After graduation our alumni have gone on to excel in diverse fields. They remain tied to the club, often returning to judge, debate, advise and hang out at various club events.
