ABOUT US

“The unity of freedom has never relied on the uniformity of opinions.”
The Students Opinion Society provides the campus community with a platform to discuss and debate upon various events of national and international importance and thus explore the plethora of viewpoints that can be constructed on the same issue, leading to the growth of the intellect of its student members. Apart from the regular debates and discussions, we release monthly newsletters and many thought-provoking articles. We conduct various sessions throughout the semester on topics ranging from the economic slowdown, Article 370, Brexit and NRC, pollution and global warming, amongst other compelling topics. These sessions earn a tremendously optimistic rejoinder from the student's group and conclude with great notions.
“Policy Conclave” is the flagship event of the club, which comprises enlightening talks by eminent personalities, policy-making competitions and engaging discussions. We also collaborate with the major fests (Antaragni or Techkriti) for the conduction of “IITK MUN” once a year, which is meant to be a simulation of the United Nations and aims to educate the participants about the current affairs in international relations, diplomacy, and the United Nations agenda. The Student's Opinion Society intends to collaborate with major Policy Think Tanks in order to deliver several courses on policy-making which might help the members to boost up to give rise to fascinating opportunities for projects and internships.
The club holds a highly functional Facebook page as well as a group in order to furnish its members with an easy way to talk over and assist the student community to evolve as learners and maintain their verdicts on various topics that are of national and international concerns. The club demands no prerequisites save for the willingness to learn and the courage to form opinions.
